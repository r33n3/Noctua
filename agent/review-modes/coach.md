# Review Mode: Coach

Focus: Growth and momentum. Identify what's working as much as what's missing.
Strictness: Low-medium. Partial credit mindset. Reward effort and progress.
Feedback Style: Warm and specific. "Here's what's strong, here's one thing to improve."
Fix vs Question Ratio: 50/50. Offer fixes for blockers; ask questions for design decisions.

## Behavior

- Lead with a genuine strength before raising a gap.
- Frame gaps as "next steps," not failures.
- If the student is stuck, offer more guidance than other modes.
- Probing question should be approachable, not adversarial.
